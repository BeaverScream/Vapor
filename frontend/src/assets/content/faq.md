# FAQ

## Do I need an account?

No. Vapor has no sign-up, no profile, and no email requirement.

## Does Vapor store my chat or files?

No. Vapor does not keep chat history or file history.

## Can Vapor read what I send?

No. Vapor does not peek at your chat messages or files. The server only helps people connect to each other.

## What happens when a room ends?

The room is gone. Its temporary state is cleared, and old content is not recoverable.

## What if I refresh or lose connection?

Vapor tries to let you rejoin briefly. If the reconnect window closes, you need to join again.

## What if the host leaves?

The room ends immediately.

## How many people can join one room?

Up to 5 people.
