# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Plain-language promise

Vapor is built for short-lived rooms and minimal data handling.

## What we do not require

- No account
- No profile
- No email

## What we do not store

- No chat history
- No file history
- No long-term room history

When a room ends, its temporary state is cleared.

## What we do not do

- We do not peek at your chat content.
- We do not peek at your file content.
- We do not sell your room content.

## What the server does

The server helps people enter the same room and connect. It is signaling-only.

## Important limitation

Vapor is provided as-is. If a room closes or the service restarts, old room content cannot be recovered.
